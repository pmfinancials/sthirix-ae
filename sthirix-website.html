About Sthirix

Regulated Introduction. Transparent by Design.

Sthirix Financial Services LLC is a UAE mainland company licensed and regulated by the Capital Market Authority (CMA) under Category 5 for financial introduction and promotion activities. We operate under the framework established by Federal Decree Law No. 33 of 2023.

Our role is to introduce qualified clients to regulated global forex brokers and provide market information, educational resources, and promotional services — all within the bounds of our CMA mandate. We never execute trades, hold client funds, or manage investment portfolios.

As a Category 5 licensee, we are committed to full transparency about the scope and limitations of our regulated activities. Every client engagement is governed by CMA conduct of business requirements.

Regulated Activity Only

Our CMA CAT 5 license authorises introduction and promotion activities exclusively. We do not execute orders or hold client assets.

UAE Mainland Entity

Sthirix is incorporated in the UAE mainland, subject to full CMA supervisory jurisdiction and FDL33 requirements.

KYC & AML Compliant

We maintain robust Know Your Customer and Anti-Money Laundering policies in accordance with UAE Central Bank and CMA guidance.

Client-First Conduct

All promotions, introductions, and materials meet the CMA's fair, clear, and not misleading standard for financial communications.

Regulatory Compliance

Built on a Foundation of Compliance

Sthirix operates within the comprehensive regulatory framework of the UAE Capital Market Authority. Our compliance programme is aligned with FDL33 and the SCA Rulebook (Decision No. 13/R.M of 2021).

01

CMA Licensing

Fully licensed under UAE CMA Category 5 for introduction and promotion of financial services. License is time-limited, renewable, and subject to ongoing compliance requirements.

02

FDL33 Framework Compliance

Our conduct of business aligns with Federal Decree Law No. 33 of 2023, including conduct standards, disclosure requirements, and market abuse prevention provisions.

03

AML / KYC Procedures

Robust client onboarding procedures including identity verification, risk profiling, and source of funds checks, consistent with UAE Federal AML Law and CMA requirements.

04

Fair Communication Standards

All financial promotions are fair, clear, and not misleading. Risk warnings are prominently displayed in all marketing and client-facing materials.

05

Complaints Handling

Clients may submit complaints to our Compliance Officer or directly to the UAE CMA. All complaints are acknowledged within 5 business days and resolved within 30 days.
